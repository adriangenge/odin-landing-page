Colouring Book

Project Description

This colouring book was created to help new immigrant and refugee families learn about their new community in an informal and engaging way. The book was co-created with immigrant and refugee families, local illustrators, translators, and leaders involved in the area of refugee well-being. The book was displayed at the Simon Fraser University book store and was available for purchase in a buy-one-give-one model. Over 200 copies of the book were distributed.

Skills + Tools Used

  • Writing/editing
  • Co-creation
  • Audience analysis/subject matter expert interviewing
  • Working with translators
