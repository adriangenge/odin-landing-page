Informational Flyer

Project Description

This informational flyer was created to provide an overview of a research study. It aimed to take complex research ideas and terminology and simplify them into a short, digestible format. The infographic contains a chart that guides the viewer through the main timepoints of our study, a brief explanation of the methods used in the research, and a list of eligibility criteria. The infographic was created for a broad audience of researchers, healthcare professionals, stakeholders, and other interested parties. The infographic was distributed at various research conferences.
